Alexander Mcmaster

I am a writer and ecologist working creatively with the seas. A regular contributor to The Irish Times, I have also published investigative work on fishing conflicts, pollution and piracy.

I am co-director of Kosamare, an environmental NGO operating in the Ionian Sea. A professional diving instructor, underwater photographer and seafarer, I also work for the Northern Irish charity Refugee Rescue, providing emergency assistance to people in distress in the Central Mediterranean.

My roots are on the Atlantic coast of Ireland.
